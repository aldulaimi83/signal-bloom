    Design Thesis

    Why this exists

    Most monitoring tools flatten the world into neutral rows. Signal Bloom argues for a more emotional interface: one that acknowledges intensity, uncertainty, and wonder without pretending the operator is a machine.

    Signal Bloom is an experiment in making monitoring interfaces feel cinematic, strange, and alive.

    Built as a static deployment artifact with a custom-domain feel, fast load path, and room for myth-making.
